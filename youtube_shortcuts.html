YouTube keyboard shortcuts

The application of keyboard shortcuts goes beyond your computer and its programs; numerous online platforms, including YouTube, incorporate keyboard shortcuts for user convenience. Here is a collection of keys enabling you to manage and control videos on YouTube using your keyboard.

Play/Pause Video

Press the spacebar or K on your keyboard to play and pause a video.

Fast forward or rewind

For a five-second incremental fast forward or rewind, use the left or right arrow keys. If you wish to skip beyond five seconds, press J to rewind by ten seconds, or press L to fast forward by ten seconds.

Seek to 10% intervals

By pressing the number keys ranging from 1 to 9, you can navigate directly to the corresponding percentage of the video. For instance, pressing 1 will jump to 10%, 2 to 20%, 3 to 30%, and so forth.

Change playback speed

To slow down a video's playback speed, press and hold Shift, then press the comma (,) key. For an increase in playback speed, hold down Shift and press the period (.) key.

Switch the video to full-screen, theater mode, or miniplayer

By pressing F, you can toggle between full-screen and normal modes. If you wish to exit full-screen mode, simply press Esc. Switching between theater mode and normal mode is achieved by pressing T. Additionally, pressing I will toggle the miniplayer on and off.

Turn on or off closed captions and subtitles

If a video features closed captions or subtitles, you can activate them by pressing the C key on your keyboard. To disable them, simply press C again.

Turn up and down the volume

While in full-screen mode, utilizing the up or down arrow keys adjusts the video volume. If you continuously hold down the down arrow key while decreasing the volume, it will eventually mute the sound. Alternatively, pressing the M key on the keyboard serves to mute and unmute the volume.

Switch chapters

Hold down the Ctrl key and, while doing so, press the left or right arrow keys to navigate to the previous or next chapter in the video.

Change frames

When the video is paused, using the comma (,) and period (.) keys navigates to the previous and next frames, respectively.
