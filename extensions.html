Chrome Extensions

Chrome extensions are small software programs that enable users to customize their browsing experience. With your permission, those installed from the Chrome Web Store can modify settings for an enhanced experience, affecting aspects such as your homepage, new tab page, search engine, or start page. It's important to note that Chrome extensions are specific to the Chrome browser, although similar functionalities may exist in other browsers.

Explore the following list for helpful and top-notch extensions that you should consider downloading.

How to install.
  1. Open up Google Chrome.

  2. Enter the URL "chromewebstore.google.com" into the address bar and hit Enter.

  3. Click on the extensions tab.

  4. Locate the extension you wish to install and click on it. Alternatively, you can utilize the search bar for this task.

  5. Press the "Add to Chrome" button.

  6. A confirmation box will appear; click on "Add extension."

  7. The extension is now installed! Click on the small puzzle piece icon to manage extensions, and use the pins to pin extensions to your screen.

Adobe Acrobat: PDF edit, convert, sign tools

Adobe Acrobat PDF tools serve as an extension that enables you to edit PDFs directly in your browser. This extension provides various tools, including options to view, fill, comment, sign, and experiment with conversion and compression features.





Boxel Rebound

Experience over 50 levels of exhilarating box-jumping madness in Boxel Rebound! This fast-paced arcade game challenges you to navigate obstacles and achieve the lowest possible time. Dive into the excitement and unleash your creativity by designing and sharing your own levels.





Google Translate

Google Translate stands as a multilingual neural machine translation service designed by Google to effortlessly translate text, documents, and websites from one language to another. This extension facilitates easy translation viewing as you browse the web. Simply highlight or right-click on a section of text and click on the Translate icon adjacent to it to translate it into your preferred language. Alternatively, for translating the entire page you're visiting, click the translate icon on the browser toolbar.





Google Dictionary

Google Dictionary serves as Google's online dictionary accessible through the "define" operator and similar phrases in Google Search. This dictionary service is also conveniently available as this Google Chrome extension. You can either double-click a word to select it, prompting a pop-up with the definition, or click on the extension icon to manually enter a word. It provides both definitions and synonyms for a comprehensive understanding of the selected word.
